    RULES

  1. Minimum four players in a round
  2. Move your player using the cursor to avoid whoever is it
  3. if you are 'it' for 30 seconds you're OUT!
  4. Tag another player before your timer hits 30
  5. Last Dot Standing WIns
  6. Box Shrinks with each Death
